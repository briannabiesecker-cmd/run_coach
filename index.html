Privacy & data
Quick read before we start

What we collect: Race goals, weekly mileage, screenshots you upload. (Later, with your permission: Strava run summaries — never GPS routes.)

Where it goes: Your training plan is stored in your browser only. Form data and screenshots are sent to Google Gemini once to build your plan, then forgotten by us.

What you can do: Click "Start a new plan" any time to delete everything. We have no account for you — clearing your browser deletes you from the app entirely.
